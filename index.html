about me

hiya, i'm lunark. my friends know me as Katelyn. i do music and stuff !!

i'm a transfem girl. i've been making things for as long as i can remember — games, code, music. right now it's mostly music.

the way i make music is by focusing on the way things make me feel. without an image or feeling, i can't make music.

my work

i make hardcore / breakcore music using FL Studio. every track is my own direct interpretation of an image, feeling, or experience in music form.

i have a long and rather tedious past with coding in C# and game development in the Unity Engine. i've since moved to music, as game design brought me a lot of mental friction with little gain.

"Each day that goes by brings me closer to the end... I'm just an old man clinging to his brushes."
things you should know

if you're going to be close to me, read this.

patience goes a long way with me.
i know i'm complex, and i know it's easy to get frustrated with me. but yelling at me will only make it worse.
i need reassurance.
silence reads as danger to me, even when my brain knows better. i won't assume the worst, but i tend to brace.
small things can knock me down.
being ignored in a groupchat when i'm genuinely asking something gets to me more than it probably should.
i rely on love and affection a lot.
praise, warmth, being seen. without it, i don't know how to value myself.
i feel alone a lot.
i'm an incredibly complex person, and can do things that end up causing the opposite of what i want long-term.
closeness makes me more sensitive, not less.
as i get closer to people, my guard lowers. that means teasing can feel like stabs if i stop bracing.
i'm bad at communicating hurt.
if you hurt me, i don't know how to speak about it. i tend to mask it, pretend i'm fine, and sometimes go non-verbal.
i go above and beyond in ways i know how.
you very likely won't notice, but i constantly push myself to make people feel seen and loved, even at my own expense.
little things matter.
you can comfort me, but all it takes is a little inconsistency and it all comes toppling down. consistency over comfort.
